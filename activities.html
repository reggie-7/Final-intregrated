Summary of our Activities

Indulge in unforgettable shared experiences at Couples Resort, where every activity is designed to bring you closer together. Glide across Soyer's Lake in tandem kayaks at sunrise, challenge each other to a friendly tennis match surrounded by wilderness, or unwind with side-by-side spa treatments as loons serenade your private session. Our carefully curated adventures include paddleboarding to hidden coves, guided forest bathing walks, and private bonfire setups under starry skies - all with premium equipment provided. For the ultimate romantic moment, don't miss our signature sunset champagne cruise, where you'll toast to love aboard a private boat with gourmet treats. Whether you're seeking thrilling water sports or peaceful nature connections, every experience is crafted exclusively for couples, with no crowds and all the magic of Ontario's most romantic lakeside setting.

(All activities included in your stay. Reserve through your cabin concierge.)
