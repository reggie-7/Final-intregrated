Summary of Chateaus

Bathed in soft, natural light from floor-to-ceiling windows, the suite harmonizes rustic Canadiana charm with modern sophistication. Reclaimed cedar beams frame the vaulted ceiling, while a stone fireplace (pre-stacked with birchwood) casts a warm glow over plush furnishings in muted tones of slate, ivory, and forest green. Handwoven Indigenous artwork adorns the walls, celebrating the region’s heritage, while a curated selection of candles infused with local pine and lavender scents adds an intimate, sensory touch.

Beyond the Suite
Guests enjoy exclusive access to the resort’s floating lakeside sauna, couples’ massages in a private forest cabana, and gourmet dining at The Timbered Rose, where locally foraged ingredients meet world-class culinary artistry.

Ideal for honeymoons, anniversaries, or rekindling romance—where the wild meets the wondrous.
